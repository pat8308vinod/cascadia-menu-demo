Campus Spaces and Places

Our buildings are nestled between the conifer forest on the hillside and the wetland of North Creek in the lowlands. Sculptures, 27-mile bike and walking path, gardens, a reading room looking over the wetland, a dining pavilion, and more. And it’s all fully accessible.
